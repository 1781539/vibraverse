About Us

Henan Jiuqian Cultural Media Co., Ltd. is a cross sea organization that integrates short videos and live broadcasts for one-stop cultivation and incubation. It focuses on the signing and cultivation of anchors/influencers, helping them solve problems such as slow start and lack of supervision, and is committed to realizing the diverse commercial value of anchors and influencers.

Jiuqian Media entered the TikTok Entertainment Guild in 2021, opened the Dubai Local MCN Guild in 2022, joined the TikTok Shop SA MCN organization in 2023, and formed a local agent team in Egypt in 2024, successively authorized to stay in the TikTok Shop UK MCN and US MCN.

Jiuqian Media has established a relatively complete operational system for overseas influencers. Through continuous accumulation and accumulation of the team, several high-quality e-commerce influencers have been incubated, producing multiple popular content and products, signing contracts with over 10000 influencers, cooperating with over 15000 influencers, with an annual traffic exposure of over 5 billion times. Currently, there are multiple offline influencer incubation bases in Henan, Hainan, Dubai, Saudi Arabia, Cairo, Egypt, and other places in China.
